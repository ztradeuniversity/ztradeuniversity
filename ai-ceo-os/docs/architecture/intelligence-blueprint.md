# Master AI CEO Intelligence & Decision Engine Blueprint

Full content delivered in chat during Prompt 1, Step 4 (2026-07-11). Permanent on-disk pointer.

## Central reconciliation

The prompt requested 12 independent "mentors." Business Architecture ¬ß3 (3A) already ruled this
out explicitly: multiple independent AIs "would give conflicting advice and fragment memory."
Resolved as **12 domain lenses across the 4 existing registers** (CEO, Mentor, Coach, Analyst),
sharing one memory (`coaching_memory`) and one evidence standard. See the full lens-to-register
mapping table in the chat transcript.

## New synthesis this step actually added (not restated from elsewhere)

1. **Decision Engine** ‚Äî a 9-step reasoning kernel (collect‚Üívalidate‚Üíconfidence‚Üíconflict-check‚Üí
   Pareto-rank‚Üíestimate‚Üíaction-class‚Üíoutput-card‚Üíreview-schedule) that every register/lens runs
   internally. Six action classes defined: act-now, sequence-later, automate, delegate (flagged
   Future ‚Äî no team until Wave 5), postpone, eliminate.
2. **Priority Engine** ‚Äî explicitly reuses the locked 2C Universal Scoring Engine, no new formula.
   "Founder energy" defined narrowly as Core-block-overrun history, not biometric.
3. **Founder Behavior Engine** ‚Äî all 15 requested behaviors mapped onto the existing 6 gap classes
   as causal sub-tags (perfectionism/procrastination/focus-drift = Execution drift with a
   diagnosed cause; decision-fatigue = extended burnout signature; strength/weakness patterns
   route to Knowledge Base, not treated as a gap at all).
4. **Notification Intelligence** ‚Äî concrete suppression logic: Critical and Approval-Required
   notifications are never suppressed, even in "founder focus protection" mode during an active
   Core/Focus session.
5. **Learning Engine boundary** ‚Äî the load-bearing rule: learning adjusts *which* recommendations
   surface, never *what the rules are*. Locked 2C weights and 3A business rules are permanently
   outside the Learning Engine's reach ‚Äî Structural-change-gated only.
6. **Daily Loop** ‚Äî expanded to the full 13-stage cycle (observe‚Üícollect‚Üíanalyze‚Üíprioritize‚Üí
   coach‚Üírecommend‚Üíapprove‚Üíexecute‚Üímonitor‚Üíreview‚Üílearn‚Üíimprove‚Üírepeat), with a concrete stopping
   condition: the burnout signature pauses new-recommendation generation during the protected
   Sunday recovery period.
