MT5 Bot Installation Guide
--------------------------

Step 1 — Open MetaTrader 5

Step 2 — Click File -> Open Data Folder

Step 3 — Open MQL5, then open the Experts folder

Step 4 — Copy the .ex5 file from this bundle into the Experts folder

Step 5 — Restart MetaTrader 5

Step 6 — In the Navigator panel, expand "Expert Advisors"
        You should see your bot listed there.

Step 7 — Drag the bot from Navigator onto any chart

Step 8 — In the dialog that appears, ensure "Allow Algo Trading" is
        ticked, then click OK

Step 9 — On the toolbar, click the "Algo Trading" button so it turns
        green. The bot is now live.
