How to Create Your Account With Us

Complete 30-Step Guide

Follow every screenshot and sequence.

Step 1 of 30
Important Instructions
  • Create your account using Chrome or another browser with our link.
  • Do NOT create your account directly from the mobile app.
  • If the app keeps opening, enter the Referral Code manually.
  • Use a NEW Gmail if it was previously used for another Exness account.
  • Use your original network SIM for OTP. Not Network converted.
1

Open the registration page through our official referral link, enter your email, choose your country, create a password, and verify your email address. This is the foundation of your trading account.

2

Enter Your Personal Information

Provide your legal name, date of birth, phone number, and residential address. This information must exactly match your official government ID — no abbreviations or nicknames.

Steps 9 – 14
09
Personal Info

Enter Your Full Legal Name

Enter your full legal name exactly as it appears on your ID.

Must exactly match your ID.
10
Personal Info

Enter Your Date of Birth

Enter your date of birth. You must be 18 or older.

11
Personal Info

Enter Your Phone Number

Enter your active phone number with the country code. It must receive SMS.

12
Personal Info

Enter the SMS Verification Code

Enter the OTP code sent to your phone by SMS.

Enter the OTP quickly.
13
Personal Info

Enter Your Residential Address

Enter your full home address, city, and postal code.

14
Personal Info

Review & Confirm Your Address

Check your address is correct, then confirm.

3

Complete Your KYC Verification

Upload your identity documents for Exness's Know Your Customer (KYC) verification. This is a regulatory requirement. Clear, high-quality photos are essential — poor photos are the most common cause of delays.

Steps 15 – 20
15
KYC

Navigate to the Verification Section

Go to the Verification section in your Personal Area.

16
KYC

Select Your Document Type

Choose your ID type. National ID Card is fastest.

17
KYC

Upload ID Card — Front Side

Upload a clear front photo of your ID. Show all four corners.

No glare or shadows. Keep the full card visible.
18
KYC

Upload ID Card — Back Side

Upload a clear back photo of your ID.

19
KYC

Take Your Selfie Photo

Take a clear selfie. Remove sunglasses or hats.

Some regions require your ID visible in the selfie.
20
KYC

Verification Submitted

Your documents are submitted. Approval usually takes under an hour.

4

Create Your Trading Account & Make Your First Deposit

Set up your first live trading account, choose your account type, leverage, and currency — then fund the account using your preferred payment method.

Steps 21 – 26
21
Trading Account

Access Your Personal Area

Log in to your Exness Personal Area (pa.exness.com).

22
Trading Account

Create a New Trading Account

Tap New Account. Choose MT4 and a Standard account.

23
Trading Account

Select Account Type & Set Leverage

Choose Standard account. Set leverage to 1:100 and currency to USD.

Leverage can be changed later from your account settings.
24
Deposit

Navigate to Deposit Funds

Tap Deposit next to your new trading account.

25
Deposit

Choose Your Payment Method

Choose a payment method — bank transfer, JazzCash, Easypaisa, or others.

26
Deposit

Enter Amount & Confirm Deposit

Enter your deposit amount and complete the payment.

Most methods credit within 15 minutes.
5

Download Platform & Start Trading

Download MetaTrader on your device, log in with your account credentials from the Personal Area, and select the correct Exness server. Your live trading account is now active.

Steps 27 – 30
27
Platform

Download MetaTrader (MT4 or MT5)

Download MetaTrader 4 or 5 from your Personal Area.

28
Platform

Install & Open the Platform

Install the app, then open it to log in.

29
Platform

Enter Your Login Credentials

Enter your account number, password, and server name in MetaTrader.

Your trading account password is different from your Personal Area password.
30
Complete

You're Live — Start Trading!

Congratulations! Your account is live and ready to trade.
