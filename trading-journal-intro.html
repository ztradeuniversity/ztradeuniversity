Example Review

A look at how a submitted trade becomes real, actionable feedback.

Demonstration examples only — your reviews are personalized to your real trades.

BUY GOLD (XAUUSD) Take Profit Hit
Entry2,382.50
Stop Loss2,375.00
Take Profit2,398.00
Result+1.8R
Mentor Review

Solid setup — you waited for confirmation at the key support zone before entering. Your risk-to-reward was well planned. Next time, consider trailing your stop once price clears the previous swing high to lock in more.

AI Review

Entry aligned with bullish market structure and a clear demand zone. Position size matched your 1% risk rule. Your London-session entries show a 68% win rate — consider prioritizing this session.

SELL EURUSD Stop Loss Hit
Entry1.0850
Stop Loss1.0880
Take Profit1.0790
Result-1.0R
Mentor Review

A good example of a valid setup that simply didn't work out — structure was correct, but you entered right before London open, which increased volatility risk. Not every loss is a mistake. Keep documenting these; the pattern will guide you.

AI Review

Trade matched your strategy criteria, but entry timing occurred 40 minutes before a high-impact news release. Suggestion: add a pre-trade news-calendar check to your checklist to avoid unplanned volatility.
